About DiffText

A fast, free, browser-based text diff tool. Paste two versions of any text and instantly see what changed — highlighted by word, line, or character.

  • Three diff modes with distinct color highlights
  • Light & dark theme, remembers your preference
  • 100% client-side — nothing leaves your browser
  • Keyboard shortcut: Ctrl+Enter to run diff

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS & JavaScript using an LCS-based diff algorithm.

Privacy

No data is collected, stored, or transmitted.

Everything you type stays in your browser. DiffText runs entirely client-side — there is no server, no analytics, and no cookies beyond your saved theme preference in localStorage.
